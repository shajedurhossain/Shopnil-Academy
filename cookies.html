01What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website stores on your device. They help sites remember your preferences and understand how the site is used. Some are essential; others are optional.

02Why we use them

We keep our cookie use minimal. We use cookies to make the site work, remember basic preferences, and measure traffic in aggregate so we can improve our courses. We do not use cookies to build advertising profiles or track you across other websites.

03Cookies we use

TypePurposeOptional?
EssentialCore site functionality and security.No
PreferencesRemember small choices, like dismissing a notice.Yes
AnalyticsAggregate traffic measurement via Google Analytics.Yes

04Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by services we use, such as Google Analytics (for traffic measurement) and embedded video players (when you play a lesson video). These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

05Managing cookies

  • Every major browser lets you view, block, or delete cookies in its settings.
  • You can install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on to disable analytics.
  • Blocking essential cookies may stop parts of the site from working correctly.

06Changes to this policy

If our cookie use changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

01Overview

Shopnil Academy ("we", "us") runs a free online learning platform. We believe education should be open — and that respecting your privacy is part of that. This policy explains, in plain language, what information we collect, why, and the choices you have.

The short version: You can browse and learn without an account. We don't sell your data. We use basic analytics and respectful sponsorships to keep the lights on — nothing more.

02What we collect

  • Information you give us. If you sign up for course updates or contact us, we collect your name and email — only what you type into the form.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we log basic technical data (browser type, approximate region, pages visited) through analytics to understand what's working.
  • Cookies. Small files that help the site function and let us measure traffic. See our Cookie Policy.

We do not require a credit card, and we don't ask for sensitive personal information to access free lessons.

03How we use your information

  • To send course updates or launch announcements you asked for.
  • To reply to your messages and support requests.
  • To understand how courses are used so we can improve them.
  • To keep the platform secure and working properly.

We will never email you marketing you didn't ask for, and every update email includes a one-click unsubscribe.

04Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure traffic in aggregate — for example, how many people started a lesson. This helps us prioritize what to build next. You can opt out of analytics cookies at any time; full detail lives in our Cookie Policy.

05Advertising & sponsorships

Shopnil Academy is funded by clearly-labeled, non-disruptive sponsorships rather than subscriptions. Sponsored placements are marked. We don't run invasive ad networks that track you across the web to build advertising profiles. If you'd like to support the platform, see Advertise with us.

06Sharing & selling

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that help us run the site (such as our analytics and email tools), and only to the extent they need it to provide that service. We may disclose information if required by law.

07Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected — for example, your email stays on our update list until you unsubscribe. You can ask us to delete it sooner at any time.

08Your rights & choices

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete it.
  • Unsubscribe from emails with one click.
  • Opt out of analytics cookies.

To exercise any of these, email [email protected] and we'll respond promptly.

09Children’s privacy

Our courses are open to learners of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without a parent or guardian's involvement. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll remove it.

10Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, note it on the site. Continuing to use Shopnil Academy after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email [email protected] or use our contact page. We read everything.

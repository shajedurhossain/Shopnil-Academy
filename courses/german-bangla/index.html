From "Hallo" to your first real conversation.

By the end of A1, you'll be able to introduce yourself, ask basic questions, and survive in everyday German situations.

    • ✓The German alphabet (das Alphabet), pronunciation, and unique sounds (ä, ö, ü, ß)

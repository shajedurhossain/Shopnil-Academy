Our mission

Education shouldn't have a price tag — or a paywall.

Shopnil Academy exists to make high-quality learning available to anyone with a phone or computer — completely free, no asterisks, forever.

A simple promise wrapped in a crest.

Green for growth. Red for passion. Gold for what you'll earn. The crest is our reminder that learning is a serious craft — and that everyone deserves a seat.

Our story

It started with a frustrating bill.

Shopnil Academy was founded in Dhaka after our founder tried to enroll a younger sibling in a $400 online programming course — and realized that for most families in Bangladesh, that's a month's rent.

Quality education shouldn't depend on your zip code or your parents' bank account. So we built something different: a platform where every course is genuinely, permanently free — funded entirely by tasteful sponsorships and ads — taught by working practitioners who care.

Today, more than 10,000 students from 40+ countries learn with us every month. Not a single one has paid a rupee, taka, or dollar.

How "free" actually works

If you're not paying, how do we keep the lights on?

Fair question. Here's the honest breakdown — and why our model means we'll never have to start charging you.

  • 📢
    Display & sponsored ads (80%) Hand-curated sponsors and AdSense. We block any ad that's deceptive, NSFW, or annoying.
  • 🤝
    Brand partnerships (15%) Tasteful sponsorships from companies that align with our mission — clearly labeled.
  • 🎁
    Optional donations (5%) Some students choose to tip — completely optional, nothing locked behind it.
  • 🚫
    What we'll never do Sell your data. Charge for courses. Put core lessons behind a paywall. Promise.

Quick answers

You might find your answer here first.

The most common questions we get — answered honestly.

Are the courses really 100% free?
Yes. Every single course on Shopnil Academy is free, with no hidden upgrades, locked lessons, or "premium" tiers. We're funded entirely through tasteful, hand-vetted advertising and optional donations — not subscriptions.
Do I get a certificate when I finish?
Yes — every course offers a shareable certificate on completion that you can put on your CV, LinkedIn, or portfolio. The certificate is also free.
How do you make money if courses are free?
We rely on respectful, clearly-labeled ads and sponsor partnerships. Every ad slot on the platform is marked "Sponsored," and we manually approve every campaign — no deceptive ads, no malware, no junk. Read the full breakdown on our About page.
Can I download lessons for offline learning?
Yes. All video lessons can be downloaded for offline study, which means you can keep learning on slow or no internet connections. We believe learning shouldn't depend on bandwidth.
I want to teach a course on Shopnil. How?
We'd love that. Email teach@shopnilacademy.com with a short pitch of what you'd like to teach and a sample of your work. We respond to every application within a week.
Do you offer live classes or just recordings?
Both. Most courses have weekly live Q&A sessions with the instructor, plus all lessons are recorded so you can catch up at your own pace. Live sessions are also recorded for everyone who couldn't make it.

Or just follow along on social.

We post free mini-lessons, study tips, and behind-the-scenes content every week.

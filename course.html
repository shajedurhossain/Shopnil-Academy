Built for Bangla speakers, by a Bangla speaker.

Most German courses online are taught in English — which means you're learning two languages at once. This course explains German in Bangla, with examples from situations Bangla speakers actually encounter: studying in Germany, working with German colleagues, or just exploring the language.

What you'll learn

  • ✓The German alphabet, pronunciation, and basic sentence structure
  • ✓Introduce yourself, order food, ask for directions
  • ✓All key grammar — articles, cases, verb conjugation, tenses
  • ✓Read German signs, menus, and short news articles
  • ✓Hold real conversations on everyday topics
  • ✓Common mistakes Bangla speakers make — and how to avoid them
  • ✓Prepare for German universities and visa interviews
  • ✓Reach B1 level (comfortable conversation) with consistent practice

Requirements

  • 🇧🇩Comfortable reading and listening to Bangla
  • 📱A phone, tablet, or computer with internet
  • 🧠No prior German knowledge — we start from absolute zero
  • ⏱About 3–5 hours per week, ideally a little every day
  • 📓A notebook (recommended) — writing helps retention

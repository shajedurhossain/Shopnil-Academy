Our promise

The rules we won't break.

We will always label sponsored content, never use disruptive formats (no pop-ups, interstitials, or auto-playing sound), and never sell learner data or use invasive cross-site tracking to target ads. If a placement would hurt the learning experience, we won't run it — full stop.
